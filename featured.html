Our Favorites

Pikachu

Pikachu can blast anything it comes across with a powerful jolt of electricity.

Beedrill

Beedrill is extremely territorial, and when threatened, flies at high speeds and attacks with large, venomous stingers.

Bulbasaur

By soaking up the sun's rays, the seed on Bulbasaur’s back grows progressively larger to power up.

Squirtle

Squirtle’s most powerful asset is his shell, allowing him to swim at high speeds (as it minimizes water resistance), and shielding him from his enemies.

Charizard

Charizard’s ability to fly and breathe fire is one of the most deadliest of combinations. It’s constantly searching for his next opponent to battle, so beware! Its fire can melt anything in its path.

Mewtwo

Mewtwo was created by science, capable of telepathy, levitation, and mind control. It is hyper-focused on defeating its foes and is known to have the most savage heart of all Pokemon.

Jigglypuff

Don’t be fooled by Jigglypuff’s cute and unassuming exterior, for its more powerful than it seems! Jigglypuff sings its opponents to sleep, and is able to mesmerize its foes with its eyes.
